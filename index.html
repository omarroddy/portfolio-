About my self

My Name is Issaka Haruna, and I was born in Ekumfi Narkwa, a beautiful town in the Central Region of Ghana. Growing up in this serene coastal community shaped much of who I am today. Instilling in me the values of hard work, respect, and determination. I come from a close-knit family of eight.
My father, Mr. Mohammed Issaka, and my mother, Madam Vida Quansah, have been my greatest sources of guidance and inspiration. I am blessed with five wonderful siblings, and together we share many fond memories of our childhood in Ekumfi Narkwa. Being born into an Islamic family, my faith has played a significant role in my upbringing. It has taught me the importance of discipline, humility, and compassion toward others.

My educational Background

My educational journey began at Ekumfi Narkwa Primary and Junior High School, where I laid the foundation for my academic growth.I later attended Christian Star International School, which further nurtured my passion for learning.
For my secondary education, I enrolled at Ekumfi T.I. Ahmadiyya Senior High School ,where I developed a strong interest in business and social studies.

My career Aspiration

As I look to the future, I am excited about the possibilities that lie ahead. My career aspiration is to become a successful entrepreneur, leveraging my skills and knowledge to make a positive impact in my community and beyond. I am particularly interested in ventures that promote sustainable development and social responsibility.
In addition to my entrepreneurial goals, I am also passionate about continuous learning and personal growth. I believe that staying curious and open-minded is essential for success in any field.

My Hobbies and Interests

Outside of academics, I have a variety of hobbies and interests that keep me engaged and inspired. I enjoy reading books on personal development, entrepreneurship, and history. These genres not only broaden my knowledge but also provide valuable insights into different perspectives.
Additionally, I have a keen interest in technology and digital innovation. I often spend time exploring new gadgets and software applications, as I believe that staying updated with technological advancements is crucial in today's world.
In my free time, I also enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking and playing football with friends. These activities help me stay active and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Conclusion

In conclusion, my journey from Ekumfi Narkwa to where I am today has been shaped by my family, education, and aspirations. I am grateful for the support and guidance I have received along the way, and I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead. With determination and a clear vision, I am confident that I can achieve my goals and make a meaningful contribution to society.
